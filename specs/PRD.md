# Product Requirements Document

## Product

AssessmentOS is an AI-powered assessment operating system for schools.

It helps teachers and academic coordinators create, import, normalize, organize, validate, review, and export exams, assignments, worksheets, answer keys, rubrics, and school-specific templates.

## Positioning

AssessmentOS is not just an AI exam generator. It is B2B SaaS for:

- Institutional question repositories
- Assessment workflows
- School templates
- Document-style paper creation
- Question normalization
- Validation
- Approval and review
- PDF/DOCX-ready exports

## Primary Users

- Teachers who author, organize, and assemble questions.
- Academic coordinators who manage standards, templates, validation, and review.
- School administrators who manage workspace settings, roles, and content governance.

## Core Problems

- Assessment content is scattered across documents, drives, and teacher devices.
- Schools lack consistent question metadata and reusable repositories.
- Paper formatting and school template compliance are time-consuming.
- AI-generated questions often lack governance, source tracking, and validation.
- Review and approval processes are difficult to audit.

## Product Goals

- Create a trusted institutional question repository.
- Normalize teacher-authored and imported content into structured question cards.
- Support document-style paper creation from reusable questions.
- Preserve source and usage-rights metadata for every imported question.
- Provide validation checks before export or review.
- Enable future approval workflows and auditability.

## MVP Outcomes

- A school workspace can organize teacher-authored question cards.
- A teacher can create draft papers from repository questions.
- A school can define basic templates and metadata expectations.
- The system can represent upload/import, AI normalization, answer key, validation, and export placeholders without claiming full automation.

## Success Criteria

- Specs clearly define the MVP and non-goals.
- Future implementation can proceed in small reviewable PRs.
- Architecture separates UI, API, data, AI, storage, extraction, export, and validation concerns.
